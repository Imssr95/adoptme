How do we join Indian Adoptions to begin the process of adopting?

The first step in joining Indian Adoptions is to call and speak with an Adoptive Family Coordinator about our agency and your adoption needs/wants. Our application process is designed to ensure that you feel ready to develop a partnership with us, while also allowing us the opportunity to ensure that your expectation align with ours and that both parties will be a good fit for one another. We believe this mutual assessment process is a key element in building a partnership with our adoptive families. If you have not yet spoken with one of our Adoptive Family Coordinators, please do so before completing the below application.

Parent Eligibility -

- Legally married
- Married at least 2 years
- Between 25 and 50 years of age
- no more than two children
